Our Services

Custom Tattoos

Custom tattoos are original designs created specifically for an individual client based on their personal preferences and ideas.

Cover-up Tattoos

Cover-up tattoos are used to conceal an existing tattoo that the client no longer wants visible. This can be due to a change in personal style, poor workmanship of the original tattoo, or simply the desire for a fresh start.

Portrait Tattoos

Portrait tattoos are depictions of real or fictional people, animals, or objects, in a realistic or stylized manner. These tattoos can be black and grey or full color and can be used to honor the memory of a loved one, pay homage to a hero or role model, or simply to display an image that holds personal significance.
